An idea isn't enough to code

Once we have refined your idea, we'll then work with you on turning that into a specification. A specification is what you need to have developers actually create your idea. Think of it as an instruction manual for the developer to understand your idea end to end.

This is the most important part of the process. We make sure you have thought of edge cases and nuances, but we also help "pair down" the specification, ensuring that it isn't too much for a first release.

Bringing your idea to life

Now that you have a specification, our development team will work on an estimate and timeline. Once we've had a kick off call, the development will begin.

We'll have regular - weekly or fortnightly - checkins to show you what we've been working on, usually consisting of demoes. We have an opportunity to help sculpt the development execution - within reason.

Build a great product and they will not come

This is the step everyone forgets. Now that your product is ready for usage, we'll ensure that people are aware of it. We'll drive traffic to your site, ensure that your service has things like Welcome Emails and Reporting dashboards.

Generally, we begin this process around when development startups - we partner with companies like ProductHunt to allow you to have coming soon pages and so on.
